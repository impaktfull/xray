## Intended use

xRay is a **development and QA tool only**. It is designed to be included exclusively in debug and staging builds of your Flutter application. It must never be included in production builds distributed to end users.

Including xRay in a production build:

- exposes internal network traffic, log output, and key-value storage (including secure storage) over the local network
- creates a serious security and privacy risk for your users

You are solely responsible for ensuring xRay is excluded from production builds. See [Quick Start](/getting-started) for the recommended debug-only setup pattern.
